O & M contracts are typically short-term contracts, ranging in duration from 1-5 years. We can establish a contractual agreement with the Project Company to provide operation and maintenance services for the project/facility. The simplest management contracts involve the private operator being paid a fixed fee by the awarding authority for performing specific tasks.
