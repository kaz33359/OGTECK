  1. Operation & Maintenance (O&M) Contracting

O & M contracts are typically short-term contracts, ranging in duration from 1-5 years. We can establish a contractual agreement with the Project Company to provide operation and maintenance services for the project/facility. The simplest management contracts involve the private operator being paid a fixed fee by the awarding authority for performing specific tasks. We got the expert management and front end operation & maintenance team for successful running of O & M contracts.

• Technical Personnel
• Spare parts management
• Logistics
• Preventive Maintenance
• Energy efficient operation

We are well equipped and looking forward for O& M for Oil & Gas separation facility, Gas Compression and treatment, Gas pipeline and distribution network etc. this year.
